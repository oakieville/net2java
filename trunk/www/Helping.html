In that spirit, I've added the NET2Java library code and translation files to the CVS repository. How much of the rest of the technology -like the VB and C# parsers, the tools, the couple hundred tests - I add to the project depends on how interested you are in it. I may change the licence depending on what you tell me you want from this project. Right now I just defaulted to the JPL (yes I work at Sun !) so you can play with it.

If you'd like to help,  I'd  love for you to drop me a note and let me know what your interest is, how you'd like to use the technology, and what kind of help you could give.
